Sol Haidong Gumdo Club

Welcome to Sol!

Haidong Gumdo is a Korean sword art which draws from battlefield tactics and techniques to build a curriculum for mental, physical and spiritual development.

There is much for students to learn - forms, step drills, meditation, sparring and cutting techniques to name but a few. Powerful, complex and beautiful, Haidong Gumdo is a wonderful style which can be pursued throughout life.

Haidong Gumdo is a worldwide martial art with over a million practitioners and over 500 training centers in 50 countries.

If you are looking for a new way to get fit and want to try something a bit different, this could be just what you are looking for. New members are always welcome. See the classes page for our current class timetable.
