Instructor

Instructor Emmanuel Clarke began training in HDGD in 2005 with Master Dorim Kim President and Head Master of the Irish Haidong Gumdo Assoication

Instructor Clarke has won Gold and Silver medals at the European Haidong Gumdo Championships 2007.

Venue

Classes are held regularly at the Iyengar Yoga Centre in Dublin.
