Welcome to Dublin Haidong Gumdo Club!

Dynamic Korean Sword Art

Haidong Gumdo is a Korean sword art which draws from battlefield tactics and techniques to build a curriculum for mental, physical and spiritual development.

Students learn forms, step drills, sitting and moving meditation, sparring as well as bamboo cutting. Powerful, complex and beautiful, Haidong Gumdo is a wonderful style which can be pursued throughout life.

Haidong Gumdo is a worldwide martial art with over a million practitioners and over 500 training centers in 50 countries.

European Championships 2007

The 1st European Championships were held at Barr in Switzerland from 15th-17th June 2007

Dublin HDGD Instructor, Emmanuel Clarke, won Gold in the Men's "Bamboo Cutting" and Silver in the "Putting out Candle" competitions.
